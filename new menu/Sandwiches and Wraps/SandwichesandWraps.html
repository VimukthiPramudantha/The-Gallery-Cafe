Sandwiches and Wraps

Ham and Cheese Sandwich

A ham and cheese sandwich features savory ham and melted cheese between slices of bread, often enhanced with condiments and fresh veggies.

Caprese Sandwich

A Caprese sandwich highlights fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil on crusty bread, often drizzled with olive oil and balsamic vinegar for a flavorful bite.

Chicken Wrap

A chicken wrap features tender, seasoned chicken wrapped in a tortilla with fresh veggies, cheese, and sauces, offering a portable and flavorful meal.

Veggie Wrap

A veggie wrap combines fresh, crisp vegetables and flavorful spreads wrapped in a tortilla, providing a healthy, light, and convenient meal option.
